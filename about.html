About LeNguyen's Garden

Orchid Garden - Phragmipedium - Paphiopedilum

LeNguyen's Garden is an orchid garden specializing in Lady's Slipper orchids found by ThienNgo N. Le in cooperation with Ken Wilson. We propagate rare Phragmipedium and Paphiopedilum species and create desirable hybrids for hobbyists. Our breeding program includes line-breeding on rare species, Phrag. kovachii influenced hybrids and white miniature compact growing hybrids. We focus on things that are rarely offered by large commercial nurseries. We grow our plants in both greenhouse and under lights conditions. So it is not a problem even you are a windowsill grower; our plants will adapt to your condition shortly.

About The Owner


ThienNgo Le - Computer Scientist - Orchids Lover

ThienNgo Le is a young computer scientist who loves orchids. He spends most of his free time with his orchids which he normally calls them his babies. ThienNgo was gifted his very first orchid (Den. farmeri) from his brother since he was 12 and he has felt in love with orchids ever since. ThienNgo's love for orchids is not just stopping at collecting and growing them; he want to do something meaningful to them. So, he got his hand on propagating and hybridizing orchids. As a result, ThienNgo has been successfully propagating rare orchid species and registering new orchid hybrids adding the diversity to the orchid world. ThienNgo loves talking about orchids and sharing his breeding project. Please feel free to contact him via his personal email if you want to chat about orchids especially slipper orchids.
